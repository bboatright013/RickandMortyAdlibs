Welcome to the Rick and Morty Adlib App! Sign Up now to get started!

Below are the rules to help you begin, have fun!

How-To Guide

  1. Draft 5 characters from the character database. These will serve as the characters in your story.
  2. Pick the story template you want to use.
  3. Fill in the form with the words your story will use.

Word Tips

Adjective | Describe something!
[crazy, drunk, pungeant]

Noun | A thing or things
[car, space station, vat of acid]

Verb | An action
[drive, fly, shoot]

Adverb | Describes an action
[quickly, silently, slowly]
